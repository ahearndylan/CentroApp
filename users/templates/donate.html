Centro Las Americas, Inc. (CENTRO) is a charitable organization exempt from taxation under section 501(c) (3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Contributions are deductible for Federal Income Tax purposes in accordance with applicable law. ADDITIONAL WAYS TO GET INVOLVED

CENTRO coordinates many volunteer opportunities throughout the year to enable you to be involved with the community. CENTRO offers programs and services that include:

  • Connecting people with opportunities to serve
  • Building capacity for effective local volunteering
  • Participating in strategic initiatives that mobilize volunteers to meet local need

Ways to become involved:

  • Latin American Festival
  • Elder's Program
  • Food Pantry Services
  • And many other opportunities

For more information regarding our programs, Internships and/or volunteer opportunities, please contact us at info@centroinc.org.
