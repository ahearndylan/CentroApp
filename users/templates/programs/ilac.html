Funded primarily with event sponsorship dollars and small grants, the Institute of Latino Arts & Culture (ILAC) is responsible for sharing our heritage, art, and culture with the general community. The Institute coordinates and implements several culturally-rich events throughout the year.

These include the followings:

  • Latin-American Festival
  • Hispanic Heritage Breakfast

The Latin-American Festival is held on the 3rd Saturday in August on the Worcester Common, behind Worcester City Hall.
